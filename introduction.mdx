## Welcome to EmailBison

**EmailBison** is a powerful, white-labeled email sequencing platform designed for agencies and businesses to optimize email outreach. It combines cutting-edge deliverability tools, customizable workflows, and advanced automation to help you scale effectively.

## Why EmailBison?

- **Deliverability**: Dedicated IPs and built-in verification for better results.
- **Automation**: Streamline campaigns with APIs and webhooks.
- **Scalability**: Unlimited workspaces and contacts.
