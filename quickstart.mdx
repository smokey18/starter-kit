---
title: Quick Start
---

Get started with EmailBison in just a few steps:

1. **Create an Account**: Sign up and log into your workspace.
2. **Add Contacts**: Upload and verify your contact lists.
3. **Build Your Campaign**: Create a multi-step email sequence with custom logic.
4. **Launch and Monitor**: Start your campaign and track performance in real-time.

Ready to go? Reach out to us directly at [support@emailbison.com](mailto:support@emailbison.com).
