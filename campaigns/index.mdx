EmailBison allows you to create, automate, and optimize email campaigns with ease.

## Key Features
- **Multi-Step Campaigns**: Design sequences with conditional logic for tailored outreach.
- **A/B Testing**: Test and refine email performance at every step.
- **Performance Analytics**: Track open rates, clicks, and replies in real-time.

## How to Create a Campaign
1. Navigate to the **Campaigns** tab.
2. Click **Create Campaign** and set your parameters.
3. Add emails with personalized content and conditional steps.
4. Launch your campaign and monitor performance.
