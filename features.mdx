title: Features
---

EmailBison offers a range of tools to optimize your email campaigns:

- **Email Deliverability**: Dedicated servers and IPs ensure top-tier deliverability.
- **Multi-Step Campaigns**: Design sequences with conditional logic for better engagement.
- **A/B Testing**: Experiment with email variations to maximize performance.
- **Automation**: Use APIs and webhooks to streamline workflows.
- **Contact Management**: Tag, filter, and segment contacts for precise targeting.

Explore these features to scale your email outreach effectively.
